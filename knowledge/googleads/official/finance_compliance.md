# Financial / Stock Lead Ads (Core References)

Use these official Google Ads policy pages for finance/stock lead-gen campaigns. 
Do not suggest policy circumvention. Always follow local regulations.

## Core policies
- **Financial products and services (policy hub)**: https://support.google.com/adspolicy/answer/2464998?hl=zh
- **Financial products and services disclosures (required disclosures)**: https://support.google.com/adspolicy/answer/2464998?hl=zh
- **Complex speculative financial products**: https://support.google.com/adspolicy/answer/15188218?hl=zh
- **Cryptocurrencies and related products**: https://support.google.com/adspolicy/answer/14009787?hl=zh

## Verification and certifications
- **Financial services verification (location-specific)**: https://support.google.com/adspolicy/answer/15332527
- **Relevant regulators and enforcement dates (location list and start dates)**: https://support.google.com/adspolicy/answer/12390454?hl=zh
- **Apply to advertise restricted financial products (certifications entry point)**: https://support.google.com/adspolicy/answer/16114090

## Region focus (US, Japan, South Korea, EU, India)
- Use the Financial services verification page and the regulators/enforcement list for country-specific requirements.
- The regulators/enforcement list includes several EU countries and India; South Korea has location specifics on the verification page.
- If a country is not listed in the regulators/enforcement table, follow the Financial products and services policy and check in-account notices for verification requirements.
