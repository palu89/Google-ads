## 模板 ID: APPEAL_FINANCE_STOCK_V1

### 适用场景
- 账户因"金融产品和服务政策"被封禁
- 广告因"资质未验证"被拒绝
- 股票/理财 Lead-gen 页面被判定为"误导性陈述"

### 申诉文案（中英双语版）

#### 第一部分：整改说明 (Rectification)
- **中文**：针对贵司指出的 [具体违规点]，我们已对落地页进行了全面整改：1. 增加了显著的风险提示（Risk Warning）；2. 明确标注了服务主体的资质信息与监管证号 [填写证号]；3. 删除了所有暗示"保本"或"高收益保证"的误导性词汇。
- **English**: Regarding the [Policy Violation] identified, we have completed a full rectification: 1. Added prominent Risk Warnings; 2. Clearly displayed our legal entity and regulatory license number: [License No.]; 3. Removed all misleading language suggesting "guaranteed returns" or "no-risk" investments.

#### 第二部分：资质证明 (Evidence of Authorization)
- **中文**：本公司（[公司名]）是经 [监管机构名称，如 FCA/ASIC] 授权的合法金融服务提供商。随信附上我们的营业执照及监管机构官网的查询截图。
- **English**: Our company ([Company Name]) is a legally authorized financial service provider regulated by [Regulator Name, e.g., FCA/ASIC]. Attached are our business license and screenshots from the regulator's official registry.

#### 第三部分：合规承诺 (Compliance Commitment)
- **中文**：我们已安排合规专员根据 Google Ads 政策重新审核了所有素材，确保后续投放严格遵守《金融产品和服务政策》及当地法律。
- **English**: Our compliance officer has re-reviewed all assets based on Google Ads policies to ensure future compliance with the "Financial Products and Services Policy" and local regulations.
