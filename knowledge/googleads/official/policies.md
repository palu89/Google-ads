# Google Ads Policy Center
- Prohibited content: Counterfeit goods, Dangerous products, Enabling dishonest behavior, Inappropriate content.
- Prohibited practices: Abusing the ad network, Data collection/use, Misrepresentation.
- Restricted content: Adult, Alcohol, Copyrights, Gambling, Healthcare, Political content, Financial services.
